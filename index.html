CABLE FAULT DETECTION USING IOT

Live Demo

Fault Simulation: The switches and resistors simulate different types of cable faults (open circuit, short circuit, and high resistance). Relay Switching: Based on the simulated fault, the Arduino activates corresponding relays to isolate the faulty section. Display and Indication: The LCD displays the type and location of the fault (R+, NF, B+, 2KN), and LEDs provide visual feedback. A buzzer might also sound an alert.

Line Follower

Live Demo
A line follower robot uses sensors to detect a line. Typically, IR sensors are used to see the contrast between a black line and a white surface. The Arduino processes sensor data to determine the line's position. Based on this, it controls the robot's motors to stay on the line. The robot continuously adjusts its movement to follow the detected path.

Implementation and Analysis of FIR and IIR filters in Digital Signal Processor Kit for Audio Signal Processing

This project implements and analyzes Finite Impulse Response (FIR) and Infinite Impulse Response (IIR) filters on a Digital Signal Processor (DSP) kit for audio processing. The implementation involves programming the DSP to realize the filter algorithms. Audio signals are input, filtered by both FIR and IIR filters, and then output. Analysis includes comparing the frequency responses and time-domain characteristics of the implemented filters. This allows for a practical understanding of their different characteristics and applications in audio processing.
